In my earlier post "[How to download a browser on Windows7 E](https://www.verboon.info/index.php/2009/07/how-to-download-a-browser-on-windows7-e/)” I explained how to create a simple script to download a browser. I just came across another blog article that might be useful for those that do not want or can’t use a script. The article “[Downloading a browser in E, without a browser, in 3 steps](http://www.withinwindows.com/2009/07/16/downloading-another-browser-in-e-without-a-browser-in-3-steps/)” explains how you can get access to a browser through Windows Media Player.
