Carrying your laptop with you sometimes can be a pain, and sometimes you don't even expect that you would need your laptop. Especially when you are at some one's private place and they ask you if you would mind to take a look at their PC that isn't doing well anymore.

So what you need is a USB stick with all your favorite applications and utilities , since you don't really want to start downloading and installing tools on someone elses PC. Still interested ? Then have a look at [PortableApps.COM](http://portableapps.com/)

PortableApps allows you to run a wide range of useful [applications ](http://portableapps.com/apps)directly from your memory stick such as Antivirus,  FTP Client and even OpenOffice.

If you still need a Christmas present for someone, go to the next store buy a USB stick and load PortableApps on it :-)
