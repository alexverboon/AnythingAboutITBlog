Usually when you would need a specific driver you go up to the vendors website and search for the driver download area. 

Since a while, HP provides a more convenient way to find latest drivers for your HP hardware. It's called the HP Softpaq download manager.

![](http://bp0.blogger.com/_Dz3BIkh6Pig/SE6OYglGWYI/AAAAAAAAAAY/pXjzZyh4DPM/s1600-h/hpdownloadmgr.jpg)The HP Softpaq download manager allows you to easilly navigate to your device and get a list of all availabble drivers and related software, view the release note, and with just one click, download the content that you need to your client. 

You can download the HP Softpaq manager from the following website:
[http://h20219.www2.hp.com/Hpsub/cache/509658-0-0-225-121.html](http://h20219.www2.hp.com/Hpsub/cache/509658-0-0-225-121.html)

It takes just a few seconds to install and of you go.
