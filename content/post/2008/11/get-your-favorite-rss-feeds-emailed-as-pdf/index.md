Ever wanted to have your very personal daily or weekly newspaper that does just contain the information you want ? Then go to [http://www.tabbloid.com/](http://www.tabbloid.com/). Just 3 simple steps, and you have your very personal information summary document prepared.

	
- 
Enter at least one RSS feed
	
- 
Select your delivery options (e-mail and delivery frequency)
	
- 
On schedule, you get an email with a  print-ready PDF

Thanks to Claude for sharing this one with me, it is simple but useful.
