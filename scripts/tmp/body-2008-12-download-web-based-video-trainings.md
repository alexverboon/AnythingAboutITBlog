Ever had that situation where you started looking at webcast that is being streamed and than for some reason you got distiurbed by something and can't look at it till its end ? Or you would simply like to keep that webcast on your local disk for later use. Well, given that the publisher allows you to download the video, do the following to create your local copy:

	
- Copy the URL of the video
	
- Then go to [http://keepvid.com/](http://keepvid.com/) 
	
- Copy the video URL and click the download button.

Have fun
