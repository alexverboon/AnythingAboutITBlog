Microsoft has released ACT 5.5 (Application Compatibility Toolkit). ACT 5.5 provides support for pre-RTM version of Windows7. 

  To learn more about ACT 5.5 I recommend reading  “[Windows 7 Application Compatibility Toolkit 5.5: Interview with Jeremy Chapman](http://blogs.technet.com/springboard/archive/2009/04/03/windows-7-application-compatibility-toolkit-5-5-interview-with-jeremy-chapman.aspx)”.

  Another great information source is the [Application Compatibility and User Account Control](http://technet.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/aa905066.aspx?ITPID=sprblog) site on Microsoft TechNet.
