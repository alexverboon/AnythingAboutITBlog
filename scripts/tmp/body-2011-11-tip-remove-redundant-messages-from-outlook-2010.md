The title says it all, if you’re suffering from too much e-mail Outlook 2010 provides a nice feature to get rid of redundant messages with just a few clicks. Before trying this out, I suggest you read [this article](http://office.microsoft.com/en-us/outlook-help/use-conversation-clean-up-to-eliminate-redundant-messages-HA101867736.aspx) so you understand what is happening here and if you have some time watch the demo [here](http://www.microsoft.com/en-us/showcase/details.aspx?uuid=9ba96443-dae0-4cfe-9861-e3f55266c189).
